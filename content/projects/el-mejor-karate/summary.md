The inception of EMK as graffiti persona spans Mexico City, Cairo and São Paulo. Besides sticking stickers, EMK writes.

In Cadáver Esquisito: uma história de amor intergaláctico, a chapter I wrote for <a href="/pdfs/Aids_Sem_Capa_ToddLanierLester_edit.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">AIDS Sem Capa: reflexões virais sobre um mundo pós-pandemia (2022)</a>, EMK initially speaks up to utter a hackneyed resolve that: “The head’s struggle with a new notion is the heart’s most soul-felt pain.” Whatever?!

The 1st edition EMK sticker was designed by artist Adham Bakry (Egypt), 2nd by Gustavo Marcasse (Brazil) and 3rd by Jakub Szczęsny (Poland).
